General Information & Expectations

  • You do not always have to fight in every unfriendly encounter—sometimes it would probably be best to talk to the enemies or try to run away

  • Take notes on plot events, NPCs that you meet, and anything else you think could even be remotely important; this will help to recap the previous session and to remember important details

  • It's D&D, roleplaying is important; I will try my best to role play, and I hope everyone tries their best to roleplay too
